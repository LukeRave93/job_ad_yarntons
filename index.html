Part time  ·  Birkenhead & Devonport  ·  Immediate start

We're not a chain. We're not a department store. We're a small family business that's been part of the North Shore since 1945, and we try to make our stores feel like it.

Our team genuinely knows our customers — not just their size, but their life. People come back year after year because someone here made them feel looked after. That's not something you can fake, and it's not something we can teach quickly.

We're looking for someone who already gets that. Someone who finds real satisfaction in a good fitting room conversation, who can read a customer, and who takes pride in selling something they'd actually recommend.

The role

Four days a week across Birkenhead and Devonport, including part of the weekend. The core of the job is customer service and sales, primarily in womenswear. You'll work with a carefully selected range of quality brands, get to know them well, and help customers find what's right for them.

If you've got skills in social media or website content, that's a bonus we'd love to grow over time — but it's not the job today.

Who we're looking for
Genuine experience in fashion retail
Someone who wants to be somewhere for the long haul
Personality, and a real willingness to learn
Someone who genuinely cares about clothes — our customers will notice if you don't

This probably isn't the right fit if you're looking for something short-term, or if you do your best work in a fast, high-volume environment. We're quiet in the right way — community-driven, not transactional.
